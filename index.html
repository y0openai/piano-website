CORE FEATURES

Three core features

01

Statistical Entry Condition Filter

Market conditions are automatically evaluated before entering a position.
Execution only happens when your configured conditions are met.

By reducing entries under unfavorable conditions, the initial stability of the hedge structure is improved.

Evaluation Criteria How It Works
Volatility Level Avoids entry when volatility is too high
Volume Pattern Detects abnormal volume periods
Trend State Filters out extremely biased periods

02

Auto Position Imbalance Correction

When market movements cause the spot·futures ratio to drift, AI evaluates the situation in real time and adjusts positions to restore balance.

AI operates within the boundaries you define.
You can adjust those boundaries or stop execution at any time.

Market Signal AI Response
VWAP Deviation Rebalances when price diverges from the volume-weighted average
Volume Spike Reassesses balance when volume surges unexpectedly
Range Breakout Readjusts as price breaks beyond the expected range
Volatility Convergence Settles positions once volatility stabilizes

03

Linked Balancing

Buy at favorable prices and let AI restore balance in real time — your average entry price continuously improves.

Normally we hold spot and futures at equal weight to neutralize market risk. When prices move in your favor, DCA buys additional on one side — intentionally creating an imbalance. AI detects this and rebalances the other side to restore the 1:1 ratio. Since the additional purchase was made at a better price, your average entry price automatically improves with each cycle.

① Balanced State Spot 0.05 BTC + Futures 0.05 BTC → Ratio 1:1 ② DCA Triggered (AI detects favorable price and buys additional spot) Spot 0.07 BTC + Futures 0.05 BTC → Ratio 1.4x (intentional imbalance) ③ Linked Balancing (AI adds matching futures to restore balance) Spot 0.07 BTC + Futures 0.07 BTC → Ratio 1:1 (restored, average entry price improved)
All features execute only according to your configured conditions. Piano does not make investment decisions for you. You can stop execution or change parameters at any time.
